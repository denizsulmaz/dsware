#WhoAreWe

DSWARE Science is a subsidiary of DSFIST parent company and a member of the Fundamental Changers Collective.
The vision of the organization is to provide technology, design and research services to the general audience, especially startups and NGOs. Shortly, a call will be made to expand the developer and design network. In the long term, the infrastructure and investment process for the international research centre continues. Stay Tuned!
